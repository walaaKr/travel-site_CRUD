================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING

Problem: Images not loading
Solution: Check filename spelling matches exactly (case-sensitive on Linux/Mac)

Problem: Video not playing
Solution: 
1. Ensure hero.mp4 is MP4 format
2. Check file size (should be under 20 MB)
3. Try different video from Pexels

Problem: Poster image appears stretched
Solution: Download a 16:9 aspect ratio image (1920x1080 or similar)

Problem: Download links broken
Solution: Search Pexels/Unsplash directly using keywords in this manifest
